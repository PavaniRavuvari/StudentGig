Find Flexible Work or Affordable Talent

Student Gig is a web-based platform designed to connect students with freelance job opportunities. It allows students to find flexible, part-time work and helps clients (individuals or businesses) post gigs and find affordable talent. We bridge the gap between skill and opportunity, focusing on early-career freelancers.

Join as a Student

How it Works

For Students:

  • Gain real-world experience.
  • Build impressive portfolios.
  • Earn side income.

For Clients:

  • Hire fresh talent at affordable rates.
  • Find assistance for short-term tasks.

For Colleges:

  • Encourage entrepreneurship.
  • Foster skill development among students.

Advantages & Disadvantages

Advantages:

  • Simple and student-friendly interface.
  • Easy way to gain freelance work experience.
  • Helps build early career credibility.
  • Encourages self-learning and discipline.

Disadvantages:

  • Risk of spam or unverified jobs.
  • May lack strong payment/invoicing system initially.
  • Needs moderation to prevent misuse.
  • Hard to compete with big freelancing platforms.

Our Future Vision

  • Add real-time chat with Firebase/WebSockets.
  • Launch mobile app version.
  • AI-based skill-to-job match recommendations.
  • Integrated payment gateways (Stripe/Razorpay).
  • Analytics dashboard for freelancers and clients.
  • University tie-ups for verified student portfolios.
